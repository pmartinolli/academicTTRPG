Deep into Geyer


Here are some measures to get a sense of what and how Geyer collected his books.

Breakdown of Geyer's holdings

Let us first see what the collection consist of in kind. There are some 1528 objects. The vast majority are books. Click on 'Other' to see what the rest is.

Map showing where Geyer's book originated from

We can show the place of publication for works in the collection. The bigger the line towards Vienna, the more books came from that place. Hover over the nodes to see the city name and zoom in to get a better look at Europe.

Publishing years

Lastly, we can set off the number of works against their publication year. All books together published before 1903 make up half of the collection. Hover over the bars to get an accurate count.
